# Computer Types

## Overview

This lab introduces the different types of computers and their purposes, highlighting how computing extends beyond traditional desktops and laptops. It reinforces the idea that modern computing is embedded into many everyday devices.

---

## Internet of Things (IoT)

The concept of **Internet of Things (IoT)** was introduced:

- IoT devices are **network-connected** and designed for a **specific function**  
- Examples include smart fridges, thermostats, and wearable devices  

These devices often prioritise:
- connectivity  
- efficiency  
- automation  

over raw performance.

From a cybersecurity perspective, IoT devices are often targeted in attacks due to **weak security controls**, outdated software, and limited patching capabilities.

---

## Embedded Systems vs IoT

A distinction was made between **IoT devices** and **embedded systems**:

- **Embedded systems**:  
  - Computers built into devices  
  - May not require network connectivity  
  - Example: automatic door sensor  

- **IoT devices**:  
  - Connected to a network  
  - Designed to communicate and share data  

Although embedded systems may have limited security controls, they are still fully functional computing systems and can present security risks if compromised.

---

## Why Do Computers Differ?

The key takeaway is that:

> There is no “perfect” computer—only the right computer for a specific purpose.

Examples:

- **Mobile devices**  
  - Prioritise portability and battery life  
  - Sacrifice performance  

- **Servers**  
  - Designed for reliability and uptime (24/7 operation)  
  - Used for hosting services such as websites  

- **IoT devices**  
  - Built for automation and connectivity  
  - Often limited in power and security  

---

## Key Takeaways

- Computing exists in many forms beyond traditional PCs  
- IoT devices are network-connected and task-specific  
- Embedded systems may operate independently without network access  
- Computer design is driven by purpose, not a one-size-fits-all approach  
- IoT devices can introduce security risks due to weaker protections  

---

## Reflection

This lab reinforced the idea that computing is integrated into a wide range of devices, each designed with a specific purpose in mind. While the concepts were familiar, it highlighted how different system types can introduce varying security considerations, particularly in IoT and embedded environments.
